Points system
The season points you earn for every fantasy game will be taken with you and count towards the season standing. Only teams in HLTV's official fantasy league counts.
Season points are distributed based on your finale placement in a given fantasy game. The distribution is as follows:
Top placement % Season points awarded
1% 25
5% 18
10% 15
20% 12
30% 10
40% 8
50% 6
60% 4
70% 2
80% 1
100% 0

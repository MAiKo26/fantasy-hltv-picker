HLTV: Worldwide Fantasy CS Competition Rules
The Organizer of the Competition
Better Collective A/S (the “Organizer”), CVR: 27652913, with the registered office in Toldbodgade 12, 1253 København K Denmark, owner of HLTV.org website (“HLTV”, “Website”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is holding an online HLTV CS:GO Fantasy competition (the “Competition”, “HLTV CS:GO Fantasy”) which is completely free of charge.
Eligibility
In order to participate in the Competition, you (“Participant”, “User”, “you”, “your”) must create and register your personal account on the Website on the following link https://www.hltv.org/signup.
If a Participant gets banned on the Website for whatever reason during any phase/stage of the Competition, they will not be eligible to participate.
Participants must be over 16 years old or older or have the consent of their legal guardian to be eligible to participate. When it comes to Participants who are not 16 years old or older, the Organizer reserves the right to request the proof of the consent of the Participant’s legal guardian, at its sole discretion and at any stage of the Competition.
The Competition is open worldwide; however, there are restrictions in place in Excluded Countries (as defined below). Participants who permanently reside in an Excluded Country are permitted to participate in the Competition but are not eligible to receive the cash prize due to legal restrictions currently in force.
Countries that may be excluded include, but are not limited to: Russia, Italy, Venezuela, Tunisia, Bosnia & Herzegovina, Congo, China, Ukraine, Zimbabwe, Mali, Lebanon, Turkey, Haiti, South Sudan, Iran, Belarus, province of Quebec (Canada), Brazil, Hong Kong, Kosovo, Iceland, and USA (each an “Excluded Country” and collectively the “Excluded Countries”). The Organizer may add or remove countries and territories from the list of Excluded Countries from time to time at its sole discretion in order to comply with applicable laws, regulations, regulatory requirements, guidelines, and industry codes of practice.
In case a resident of any of the Excluded Countries decides to participate and wins the prize, the Organizer will, within the limits of its capabilities, and at its sole discretion, take actions to try to provide them adequate and fair alternative as permitted by applicable law and regulation, depending on the country of residence of the particular winner. The alternative could consist of CS: GO skins in the monetary value of the cash prize, if applicable laws, regulations, regulatory requirements, guidelines, and industry codes of practice allow for it.
By taking part in the Competition, Participants must consent and fully accept the Competition Rules and the Privacy Policy posted on the Website.
Employees and freelancers hired by HLTV or another member of the Better Collective Group, directly or through intermediary companies, are not allowed to participate in the Competition.
How to participate
Participating in the Competition is completely free of charge.
To participate, you will have to draft your CS:GO Fantasy team, pick CS: GO players for each CS:GO event, and accumulate points. You can find more information and instructions about participation in CS:GO Fantasy game - Draft your fantasy team today! | HLTV.org and Introducing Fantasy | HLTV.org.
Each User can participate only once in the Competition and from one account only.
Start and Closing Date
The Competition starts on January 10, 2026.
The Competition shall end on June 21, 2026.
Selection of winners
The selection of winners will be made according to the total number of points until the end of the Competition. Prizes will be allocated according to Participant rank and number of points at the end of the season. The highest-ranked Participant with the most points will be awarded.
In case of a tie the player with most tier 1 placements will win between the tied players. If still tied, then most tier 2 placements, then most tier 3 placements, etc. If the tied players have exact same number of placements in each tier, the player with the best placement in their latest game will win.
Information on the usernames/nicknames of the Competition winners and the value of the prizes will be published and publicly available on the Website.

If the highest-ranked Participant is permanently residing in any of the Excluded Countries (as defined above in Section 1.4.) at the moment of winning, the Organizer shall award to him/her the following alternative prize if applicable laws, regulations, regulatory requirements, guidelines, and industry codes of practice allow for it.

If this is not possible due to legal restrictions, the Organizer will, within the limits of its capabilities, and at its sole discretion, take actions to try to provide him/her adequate and fair alternative, as permitted by applicable law and regulation, depending on the country of residence of the particular winner.
The Organizer will contact the winner via email connected to their registered HLTV account or HLTV direct messages to arrange handing over the prize no later than 14 days after the end of the Competition.
The Organizer will hand over the prizes to the winners in a maximum of 90 days after settling and verifying them. The winners will be asked to provide their Steam trade link or other credentials, in addition to some other information needed for the purpose of arranging the transfer of the award. For more information on what data will be requested, please read the Sections Taxation and Personal Data.
The winners must respond to the Organizer within a maximum of 30 days after they are contacted. The winner who fails to provide the credentials and other necessary info within 30 days will lose eligibility to receive the prize. In that case, the award will be handed over to the next in line Participant with the most points.
Prizes are non-exchangeable, non-transferable, and no alternative is offered.
The usernames/nicknames of the winners will be announced publicly on the Website. Players will be given the opportunity to object to their information being published by sending an email to fantasy@hltv.org.
The cash prizes will be delivered/transferred via Bank transfer, Skrill or PayPal. The CS: GO Skins prizes shall be transferred to the winner through the Steam system.
Taxation
The prize will most likely be taxable in your area. You must participate in the process of determining tax liabilities with the Organizer. It is your own responsibility to report the amounts earned and comply with the legal obligations established by the tax regulations that apply to you. Both the form of notification and the taxation of the prizes may vary depending on the country. In case the Organizer is liable to pay taxes in your jurisdiction, we will contact the local tax office in your jurisdiction, assess the local tax regulations, calculate, and pay any taxes related to the prize.
In case a cash prize was awarded to you, the Organizer shall request formal proof of the report you submitted to the tax authorities for the amounts earned prior to paying out the total amount of the award you won. As soon as you provide such evidence, the Organizer will pay out the total award to you.
The Organizer shall be at your disposal if any confirmation and/or documentation is needed from HLTV.org ApS for submitting the tax report to your local tax authorities and/or if any pre-payments are needed for this purpose.
In the event any pre-payments are needed for tax reporting reasons, those expenses shall be covered by the Organizer. The Organizer shall pay out to the winner the requested sum upon receiving such a request from the winner. Any pre-paid amount will be deducted from the total amount of the award won.
Protection of personal data
Protection of your personal privacy when you participate in this competition is very important for us. Therefore, we process your personal data with the greatest caution and in accordance with all relevant laws.
For the purpose of your successful participation in the competition, we have to process the following personal data:
Your age – we need this to assess whether you satisfy legal requirements related to the threshold age for participation in the competition (more about in the section 'Eligibility', subsection 3 in this Rules).
Your nationality and/or place of residence – we must check whether you are based in an ‘Excluded Country’ (for more info, check section ‘Eligibility’, subsection 5 in this Rules).
Your HLTV username – processing this data is necessary to distinguish you from other competitors. Also, we may publish your username when we create the ranking list and if you win the competition (more about in the section 'Selection of winners', subsection 3 in this Rules).
PII and financial data – in case you win the competition, we will ask you for your personally identifiable information (such as name, surname, address) and financial data (e.g., bank account, e-wallet details). We will need this info for delivering or paying the prize.
Please, be aware that we could have an obligation to announce who the winners of the competition are pursuant to transparency obligation regulated by relevant national legislations. In such cases, the list of the winners' usernames will be posted on the HLTV website.
The legal ground for processing your personal data is your consent given before you took part in this competition or our obligation to comply with mandatory laws.
We will keep your data until the completion of the competition. When the competition is concluded and the prize is delivered, all of the personal data we have collected for the successful realisation of the competition will be deleted. However, be aware that we will keep posts with info on competition winners in order to preserve the transparent organisation of the competition. Also, we may keep some of your personal data if it is necessary to comply with national laws.
You are entitled to exercise all data protection rights granted by the EU Law, and particularly by the GDPR. If you would like to find out more about your rights, how to exercise them, as well as more about our way of protecting your data, we advise you to read our Privacy Policy.
Breach of the rules
Any breach of the Rules is forbidden and will be sanctioned. Punishments include disqualification from the Competition, warnings about conduct, temporary bans for participation, permanent blocking from accessing the Competition, and in certain circumstances, your HLTV account suspension or a permanent ban.
Breach of the Rules may include, but is not limited to:
Participating in the Competition from multiple accounts. Every prize-winning account goes through a series of account checks to ensure that it wasn't involved in multi-accounting abuse. If the abuse is found by the Organizer, the offender will not be eligible to receive the prize.
Leagues or team names featuring obscene vocabulary or offensive remarks (or otherwise inappropriate). Please respect your fellow Participants and refrain from using abusive, racist, sexist, homophobic language or any other forms of discrimination. We also ask the Participants to avoid using any intentionally provocative content, e.g., extreme political views.
If a Participant violates the Competition Rules, they will be punished at the Organizer's sole discretion. The extent of punishment is determined by the severity of the offense and whether the Participant is a repeat offender. Cases are judged on individual merit, and decisions made by the Organizer are final and should be respected.
If a Participant gets banned on the Website for whatever reason, they will no longer be eligible to participate in the Competition.
Prizes for individual games
CS: GO Skins and other prizes awarded for individual games in the season and outside the season are subject to the restrictions described in the Breach of Rules section and are in place for games starting from June 13th, 2023, to December 31st, 2023.
Complaints
Any issues or doubts that have no clear guidelines in the above rules will be dealt with at the discretion of the Organizer’s administration.
If you wish to contest some of our decisions, you can do so by sending an email to fantasy@hltv.org.
Any legal disputes that may arise will be subject to Danish law and will be settled by the competent courts of Denmark.
Endnotes
The Organizer reserves the right to make changes to the above Rules at its sole discretion and will inform users of any significant changes by announcing the changes publicly on the Website.

FAQ
  • Where can I find rules for participation?
    You can find the rules here.
  • What is the difference between money draft and live draft?
    In money draft each user is given a starting budget and can freely choose between any player attending the tournament. In live draft each user take turns to select a player, hence a player can only be in one team.
  • How many leagues can I join?
    You can join up to six leagues in each fantasy game.
  • How many league can I create?
    You can create up to three leagues in each fantasy game, however a created league also counts in the total number of joined league.
  • How many users can join a league?
    In live draft the number of users in a league depends on the number of teams attending the event, since a player can only be picked by one user. In a money drafted league there is no limit.
  • What is padding (in round breakdown)?
    If a player advances early in the group stage of a tournament then their points from previous rounds will be extended so all players give points in an equal amount of rounds. This will be marked as padding in the round breakdown.
  • How do I invite people to join my league?
    When you create a league you will get a unique invite link which can be found in the upper right corner in the league pages.
  • How do I find users to compete with?
    You can join our public money league. To get users to join your own league, invite your friends or ask the forum.
  • Why do I get negative points when a player with bad rating has the noob role?
    The noob role only counts to your bonus, hence the player will still get points based on their rating in the match.
